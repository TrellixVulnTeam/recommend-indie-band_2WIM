How it works



This website utilizes last.fm, which is a music website containing a bunch of data about artists. Each artist gets tags assigned to them by users of the site. Luckily, Last.fm is nice enough to provide an API so developers like myself access this information.


This website takes the five artists you've given, gets their tags from Last.fm, then does some calculations to find similar artists. This is different than Last.fm's similar artists feature, and should provide different results.


Interpreting your results

On the results page, the percentages indicate why the particular artist was chosen. A match of 100% means that your given artists and this particular artist shared the same amount of that tag. Looking at the example on below, the band 'Explosions in the Sky' was chosen. They had a post-rock level of 100%, meaning that they had the same level of post-rock as your provided artists. They also had an instrumental level of 83%, meaning that they are "83% as experimental" as your provided artists. If the band seems interesting, you can click on "Go to Artists' Page", where you can read a short bio about the artist, find out what their most popular albums are, and listen to a few of their most popular songs.
